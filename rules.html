General Rules — All Account Types

No Martingale or Gambling

Martingale strategies, grid trading with no stop loss, and all-or-nothing gambling behavior are strictly prohibited on all account types.

Payout Schedule

First payout available after minimum trading days are met. Subsequent payouts available on a bi-weekly basis. Processed in 24–48 hours via UPI, crypto, or wire transfer.

News Trading Allowed

High-impact news trading is permitted across all account types. However, holding positions into extreme news events (e.g. NFP) carries significant gap risk.

Account Sharing Prohibited

Each account must be traded by a single individual. Account sharing, group trading on one account, or having a third party manage your account is strictly prohibited.

Platform: MT5

All accounts trade on MetaTrader 5 (MT5). Your login credentials, server address, and account ID will be sent to your email upon account activation.
