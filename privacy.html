This Privacy Policy should be read alongside our Terms of Service. By creating an account or using The Funded Best, you consent to the data practices described below.

1. Introduction

The Funded Best ("Company", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and platform, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights available to you.

By registering an account, purchasing a challenge, or otherwise using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account details: full name, email address, and password when you sign up
  • Authentication data: if you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google
  • Challenge & order details: the challenge type, account size, and entry fee you select at checkout
  • Payment details: UPI transaction reference / UTR numbers, cryptocurrency transaction hashes, and screenshots or images of payment proof that you upload
  • Support communications: messages you send us via the support page, email, or Telegram

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Trading & account activity: balances, profit/loss, phase progress, and other data generated as you use challenge accounts
  • Usage data: pages visited, session timestamps, and similar technical information, which may be collected via your browser and stored locally on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account, and authenticate your sign-ins
  • Process challenge purchases, verify payments, and activate funded accounts
  • Send login credentials, order updates, and important account notifications by email
  • Provide customer support and respond to your enquiries
  • Monitor trading activity for compliance with the Rules and Terms of Service, including detection of prohibited conduct
  • Maintain the security, integrity, and proper functioning of the platform
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: infrastructure providers (such as our cloud database and authentication provider) that store and process data on our behalf
  • Payment intermediaries: UPI and cryptocurrency payment processors necessary to verify and reconcile your transactions
  • Legal & regulatory authorities: where required to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or governmental request
  • Successors: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to equivalent privacy protections

5. Payment Information

We do not collect or store full card numbers, bank account credentials, or wallet private keys. Do not include sensitive financial credentials in payment proof screenshots beyond what is necessary to verify your transaction.

For UPI payments, we collect the UTR / transaction reference number and any screenshot you upload as proof of payment. For cryptocurrency payments, we collect the transaction hash and wallet address used to send funds. This information is used solely to verify and match your payment to your order.

6. Data Storage & Security

Account and order data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore database services). We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including access controls on our database.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential and for all activity that occurs under your account.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active, and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Payment proof images and transaction references are retained for verification and audit purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to the retention exceptions described above.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential processing, where applicable
  • Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 13. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage and session storage to keep you signed in, remember your checkout selections, and improve your experience. These do not constitute third-party advertising cookies. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings, though doing so may require you to sign in again or restart checkout.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18, consistent with the eligibility requirements in our Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. International Data Transfers

Our infrastructure providers may process and store data on servers located outside India. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure your information continues to receive an appropriate level of protection consistent with this Policy.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify registered users via email or a platform notification. Your continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

  • Email: support.thefundedbest@gmail.com
  • Support page: thefundedbest.com/support
  • Telegram: Join our Telegram
